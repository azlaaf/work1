our service

Invoice

Invoicing is the heart of a business! That's what it takes to run a business

Are you still using paper and pencil to make an invoice? then 2wahka is a simple and free solution for you.

In our free vision, you can create up to 100 invoices, which you can print easily and simply for your customers.

You can easily upgrade to our silver or gold version and get unlimited invoices without starting over.

You can easily create the customers you have as a regular customer, so that you do not have to fill in all the standard information all the time. In silver and gold, you will have the option to send by. Email or WhatsApp.

Salary slip

What's more irritating than pay slips???

The salary in the company is the monthly annoying work for every business owner, and time consuming.

In the free version, 2wahka saves the payslips so you can see what was paid last month or year.

With our Gold version, you can have 2wahka do the salary calculation automatically. !!!

In GOLD, there is an unlimited number of employees that can be created.

With our GOLD vision, you can automatically send the pay slip to your employees by email, or Whatsapp as a PDF

That is easy

Time registration

Companies where the owner is not always present can use time registration with great advantage.

The employee does the work!!! the owner/manager must "only" do a check

Benefit:

  • You must not monitor when the employee comes to work..
  • You can introduce flexible working hours.
  • The employee does not get paid for the time or the employee was not seen.
  • sickness, vacation and other absences are registered in the same system.
  • Automatic transfer to pay slip. (where you can always edit) in the Silver and GOLD versions
